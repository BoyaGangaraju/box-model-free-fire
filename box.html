FREE FIRE MAPS AND MAIN PLACE

FREE FIRE

Free Fire is a fast-paced mobile battle royale game developed by Garena. In each match, around 50 players are dropped onto a remote island where they have to search for weapons, armor, and supplies, and eliminate others to be the last person or team standing. The game is optimized to run smoothly even on low-end smartphones, with matches lasting about 10-15 minutes — making it ideal for quick gameplay sessions. Free Fire also features multiple modes beyond the classic battle royale, a variety of characters with unique abilities, regular updates and events, and monetization mostly through cosmetic purchases.

BERMUDA MAP

Bermuda is one of the original and most iconic battle royale maps in Free Fire. It’s a forest-themed island with a balanced mix of dense woods, open fields, hills, rivers, and urban zones. Measuring about 800 × 800 meters, it offers diverse terrains that suit different playstyles — whether you like close combat in buildings, long-range sniping from high ground, or stealthy rotations across open land. Key landmarks include the Peak (a central high point), Bimasakti Strip, Factory, Cape Town, Hangar, Mill, and Rim Nam Village. Because of its variety and familiarity, Bermuda remains a popular choice for both new and experienced players.

KALAHARI MAP

The Free Fire Kalahari map is a desert-themed, complex terrain map with high-tier loot, released in 2020. Players can find cover in its numerous buildings, trees, valleys, and rocky formations, although large open spaces exist between locations like Refinery and Command Post. Its complex terrain and high loot rewards players for their map knowledge and strategic survival.

BERMUDA REMASTED MAP

Bermuda Remastered is an updated version of Free Fire's original Bermuda map, featuring a more modern look with new locations like Nurek Dam and Aden's Creek, while retaining the core layout and familiar building placements. This remastered map also introduced new elements such as roads, updated structures, and the exclusive Scythe melee weapon.

PEAK PLACE

In Free Fire, "Peak" refers to a location on the Bermuda map, an area known for high player activity and fierce battles due to its strategic importance and numerous structures, including houses and a large tower.
